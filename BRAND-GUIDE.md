## Voice & Tone
- Whimsical but not childish
- Personal and warm — "your favorite garden faery"
- Nature-magic energy — mushrooms, sparkles, bees, flowers
- Labels stay clean and functional, magic comes through visuals
- No corporate speak, no marketing catchphrases
